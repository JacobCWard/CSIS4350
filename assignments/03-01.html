Shorthand property declarations affect each of these:

  • Margins and Padding
    • 1 value: all sides the same
    • 2 values: first- top and bottom same, second- left and right same
    • 4 values: clockwise definition starting at top
  • Borders
    • (in order) border-width, border-style, and border-color
    • Ommitted values do not matter
  • Fonts
    • (in order) font-style font-variant font-weight font-size/line-height font-family
    • font-size and font-family are required. Other properties are default if not declared.
